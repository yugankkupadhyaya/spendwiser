## FAQ Copy

### Q: Do I need to install anything?
```
No. SpendWise runs entirely in your browser. 
Just fill out the form and get your results.
```

### Q: Do you access my accounts or tools?
```
No. We never ask for API keys, passwords, or credentials.
Our audit is based on what you tell us about your stack.
```

### Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?
```
Our estimates are based on real pricing data and conservative 
heuristics. We prefer to under-promise — actual savings 
are typically equal to or greater than our estimates.
```

### Q: What tools do you support?
```
Currently 20 tools including ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Copilot, 
Jira, Linear, Notion, Slack, Figma, Docker, Kubernetes, 
AWS, and Vercel. We add new tools regularly.
```

### Q: Is my data stored or shared?
```
No. All analysis happens in your browser. If you refresh,
your data persists in local storage — but it never leaves
your machine.
```

### Q: How long does an audit take?
```
About 3 minutes to fill out the form. Results appear instantly.
```
