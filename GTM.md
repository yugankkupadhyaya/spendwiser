# SpendWise — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Product Positioning

**One-liner:** SpendWise audits your AI tool stack and finds thousands in recoverable SaaS spend — in 3 minutes.

**Elevator pitch:**
"Engineering teams are bleeding money on overprovisioned AI tools — ChatGPT Enterprise for a 5-person startup, Jira Enterprise for a 12-person team, unused seats everywhere, duplicate AI assistants nobody canceled. SpendWise audits your stack, catches the waste, and tells you exactly what to do about it. No backend setup. No API keys. Just a form and instant results."

**Tagline candidates:**
- "Stop burning money on AI tools."
- "Your AI stack is leaking money. Here's exactly where."
- "SaaS spend intelligence for the AI age."
- "The procurement audit your AI stack deserves."

## Acquisition Channels

### 1. Developer Community (Top Priority)

**Channels:** Hacker News, GitHub, Reddit (r/programming, r/devops), X/Twitter

**Tactic:**
- Launch Show HN with the audit engine
- Open-source the catalog (community-maintained tool/plan data)
- Share anonymized audit results ("We audited 100 startups' AI stacks — here's what we found")
- Write technical blog posts: "Building a deterministic recommendation engine"

**Estimated CAC:** $0.02 (organic)
**Expected virality:** High — developers love sharing audit results

### 2. Content Marketing

**Topics:**
- "How much should your startup spend on AI tools?"
- "The hidden cost of unused SaaS seats"
- "ChatGPT Enterprise vs Team: When to downgrade"
- "We analyzed 50 startups' AI stacks — here's the average waste"
- "Jira vs Linear: The real cost of enterprise project management"

**Format:** Blog posts, Twitter threads, LinkedIn posts, short-form video

### 3. Partnerships

**Potential partners:**
- Startup accelerators (Y Combinator, Techstars) — free audits for portfolio companies
- VC firms — provide audits to portfolio companies as a value-add service
- Co-working spaces — offer free audits to members
- Cloud providers (Vercel, AWS) — integration partnerships

### 4. Paid Acquisition

**Channel:** Google Ads
**Keywords:** "SaaS spend audit," "AI tool cost optimization," "reduce SaaS spending"
**Budget:** $2K/mo starting
**Expected CAC:** $15-25

## Monetization Strategy

### Free Tier (v0.1)

- Complete audit with all findings
- No login required
- Basic savings estimates
- **Goal:** Acquisition, virality, data collection

### Pro Tier (v0.3, $19/mo)

- Historical audits and trend tracking
- PDF/CSV export
- Unlimited tools (free tier caps at 5)
- Priority support
- **Goal:** Monetize active users

### Team Tier (v0.4, $49/mo)

- Multi-department audits
- Team collaboration (shared findings, notes)
- Benchmarking against anonymized peers
- Scheduled recurring audits
- **Goal:** Expand within organizations

### Enterprise (v1.0, custom)

- SSO, SAML, SCIM
- Custom tool catalogs (private/internal tools)
- API access
- Dedicated support, SLA
- On-premise option
- **Goal:** High-ticket enterprise deals ($10K+/yr)

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Current — v0.1)

**Goal:** Validate product-market fit, gather feedback
**Target:** 100 audits
**Metric:** Audit completion rate, share rate, feedback quality

**Activities:**
- Share with personal networks
- Post on Hacker News
- Reach out to 20 startup CTOs for feedback

### Phase 2: Community Launch (v0.2)

**Goal:** Organic growth through developer community
**Target:** 1,000 audits/month
**Metric:** DAU, referral rate, time to first finding

**Activities:**
- Product Hunt launch
- Technical blog posts
- Open-source announcement
- Conference talks (if applicable)

### Phase 3: Paid Acquisition (v0.3)

**Goal:** Sustainable growth through paid channels
**Target:** 5,000 audits/month, 200 paid subscribers
**Metric:** CAC, LTV, conversion rate, churn

**Activities:**
- Google Ads
- LinkedIn targeting (CTOs, VPs Engineering)
- Partnership deals
